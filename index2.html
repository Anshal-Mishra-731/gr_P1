• IIT Delhi

    The Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi) is a public institute of technology located in Delhi, India.

  • IIT Bombay

    The Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay) is a public technical institute in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

  • IIT Roorkee

    IIT Roorkee is a technical university located in Roorkee, Uttarakhand, India. It is the oldest engineering institution in India..

  • IIT Ropar

    Indian Institute of Technology Ropar (abbreviated IIT Ropar or IITRPR) is a public technical university located in Rupnagar, Punjab, India.

  • IIT Kharagpur

    Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur) is a public institute of technology in Kharagpur, West Bengal.
